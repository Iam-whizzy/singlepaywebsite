The Future of Payments

SinglePay is an online payments solution that focuses on online buyers and sellers. SinglePay is here to ensure that buyers and and sellers who are conducting business online can trust each other. Our solution works by holding the buyer's money and releases it to the seller once the customer acquires the product. This eradicates the issue of scamming for both online buyers and sellers.
